# Privacy & Data Security (Chrome Web Store Disclosure)

## Single Purpose: 
PreTab improves browser navigation by restoring your last active tab when closing the current one or following a smart history queue.

## Permissions Disclosure:
- tabs: 
Required to monitor tab switching, opening, and closing to maintain a smart navigation history.

- storage: 
Required to store your navigation preferences (e.g., MRU vs. Queue mode) locally.

Remote Code: 
None. This extension does not use or fetch any remote code.

User Data: 
No personal data, browsing history, or identifiers are collected, stored, or transmitted.

Privacy Policy: 
https://github.com/maiklindner/PreTab/blob/main/PRIVACY.md

## Test Instructions:
1. Configuration: Defaults to MRU (Most Recently Used) mode.
2. Open three different websites in three tabs (A, B, C).
3. Focus on Tab A, then switch to Tab C.
4. Close Tab C. The extension should automatically focus back on Tab A (last active) instead of Tab B.
5. Background Queue test: Open multiple links in background, then close current tab to see it cycle through the new tabs.
