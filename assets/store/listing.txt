Title:
PreTab - Previous Tab Switcher

Short Description (max 132 chars):
Automatically switches back to the last active tab when closing the current one. Improves workflow with a smart history queue.

Description:
PreTab is a lightweight browser extension that improves your browsing workflow by changing how tabs behave when closed.

It combines two smart behaviors to keep you focused:
1. MRU Switching: When you close a tab, PreTab switches you back to your last active tab instead of the nearest neighbor.
2. Smart Queue: If you open multiple tabs in the background, PreTab treats them as a queue. Closing one takes you to the next unread tab.

Features:
- Smart History: Keeps track of your tab activation order in the background.
- Batch Processing: Automatically detects background tabs and guides you through them sequentially.
- Zero Configuration: Install and it just works immediately.
- Privacy Focused: No tracking, no analytics, no external servers. 100% offline.
- Lightweight: Written in pure JavaScript with zero external dependencies.
